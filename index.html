SFG - GlycoBioInformatics satellite registration
The GlycoBioInformatics satellite meeting aims to introduce researchers to software tools and database that can assist them in their day to day work. These tools and databases can be used as information systems or for the processing and interpretation of experimental data.

In this satellite software and database providers will introduce attendees to their tools and how they can be used. During the first half of the meeting the tools and databases will be presented in short talks followed by the opportunity to asked questions. After a short break the software and database providers live demonstrate their tools to interested individuals. The participation in the satellite is free of charge - please register anyway since this will allow us to plan the meeting better.

Please note: Registration for the satellite meeting on this page allows to attend the satellite only. If you plan to participate in the SFG meeting as well please use the main registration page to sign up for the main meeting.
